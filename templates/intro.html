Perception AI : Automated Synthetic Data Generation

Step 1

Enter Data

Using either our form based entry or our cloud API for automated requests, enter the scenario you need rendered with a mix of input images, descriptions, and paramaters. Don't worry about being perfect we can assist in generating automatic permuations such as camera, lighting, and environment changes.

Step 2

Perception AI Labs Processing

The Perecption AI backend segments the job in to those that can be completely automated or those that require our backend human workforce to create your assets. This is done with our in house AI/ML tools to generate synthetic images, videos, datasets, and labels.

Step 3

Download your data sets

When the job is complete your account is notified and all assets are exposed to a download endpoint. Due to the large nature of the assets (many GB) this can be via supplied browser download manager or with optional command line tooling for automating your workflows.
